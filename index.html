Questions

Answers Worthy
of a King

Is it really all natural?

Yes — completely. Every ingredient is sourced from plants, minerals, or beeswax. There are no synthetic preservatives, no petroleum derivatives, no artificial fragrances, and no hidden chemicals. What you see on the label is exactly what's in the jar.

What SPF does Apex Crown Sentry™ offer?

Apex Crown Sentry™ uses non-nano zinc oxide as the active sunscreen ingredient, which provides broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection. Exact SPF rating varies by application thickness. We recommend generous, even application for full protection. SPF testing is in progress.

Is King's Best™ for all skin types?

Yes. Our formulas are designed to work with your skin's natural balance, not override it. The coconut oil, shea butter, and olive oil base is gentle enough for sensitive skin while still providing the protection and nourishment that active, tough-working skin needs.

How long does a jar last?

Each 2 oz jar typically lasts 4–6 weeks with daily use, depending on application area and amount. The beeswax formulation means a little goes a long way — you don't need much to get full coverage and benefit.

Can I use these products on my face?

Absolutely. Apex Crown Sentry™ is specifically formulated as a face moisturizer and sunscreen. Rise & Move™ and Rest & Restore™ are designed for body use but can be used on the neck and jaw area. Avoid eye contact with all products.

Why glass jars instead of plastic?

Because King's Best™ is a premium product and it should feel like one. Glass doesn't leach chemicals into oil-based formulas the way plastic can over time. The frosted glass jar with gold lid is not just aesthetics — it's part of our commitment to purity from the inside out.
